Hollow Knight

Hollow knight is a game about the knight ⚔, who you play as, exploring the forgotten world of Hallownest. Hallownest was once full of vibrant life, having housed many different types of bugs and creatures, but because of the infection, the once thriving civilization was turned into a wasteland. The game is riddled with dialogue and quotes, one of my favorites being:

"Be the person you would like to meet. If our life is but a spark in eyes of gods, lets blind them at least once." -unknown
